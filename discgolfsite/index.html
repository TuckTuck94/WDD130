Get Into Disc Golf

What is Disc Golf?

Disc Golf, otherwise known as Frolf, or Frisbee Golf. Is a growing sport in America in which plastic frisbees are thrown into baskets using rules similar to Golf. Players complete by throwing from a tee pad towards a basket, throwing until the disc is in the basket. Similar to Golf, the lowest score wins. And has terms such as Par, Birdie, Eagle. However, a Hole in One is referred to as an "Ace" in Disc Golf. See more info about discs here

What Equipment Is Used?

In addition to various Discs used throughout the game (which includes Drivers, Mid-Range, and Putter Discs). Players often have a bag which includes a water bottle, sports drink, or various snacks. Some players have optional tools to help retrieve their discs from hard to reach places, such as up in a tree or in a river. However, the first time player only needs a bag and a few discs to play!
