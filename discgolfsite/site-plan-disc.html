Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of this Website is to introduce others to a hobby I greatly enjoy and encourage them to partake in it as well. With the site, I plan to briefly explain the game in addition to show equipment and courses. Hopefully to encourage those around us to partake in the hobby.

Audience

The audience for this Website would be those possibly looking to get a new hobby, want more exercise, or want to find a way to have a good family activity. There is no right or wrong answer for getting into it. All they need to do is look into it!
